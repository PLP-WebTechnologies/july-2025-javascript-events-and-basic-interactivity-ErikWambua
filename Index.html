Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What are your visiting hours?

Our general visiting hours are from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM daily. However, ICU visiting hours are more restricted: 11:00 AM to 12:00 PM and 5:00 PM to 6:00 PM. Please check with the specific department for any variations.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Please contact our billing department at insurance@afyanjemahospital.com or call +254 XXX XXX XXX to verify if your specific insurance plan is accepted.

How do I get my medical records?

You can request your medical records by visiting our medical records department with a valid ID. Alternatively, you can download the request form from our website and email it to records@afyanjemahospital.com. Please allow 3-5 business days for processing.

What should I bring for my appointment?

Please bring your ID, insurance card, any relevant medical records or test results from other facilities, a list of current medications, and a form of payment for any copays or deductibles.
